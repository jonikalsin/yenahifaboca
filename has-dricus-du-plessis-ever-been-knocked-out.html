Has Dricus du Plessis Ever Been Knocked Out?

Dricus du Plessis, a prominent mixed martial artist, has had a remarkable career, competing in various organizations including the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC), Extreme Fighting Championship (EFC), and Konfrontacja Sztuk Walki (KSW). His record stands at an impressive 21-2-0.

Throughout his career, du Plessis has showcased his skills in both striking and submission, securing victories in diverse ways. But the focus here is to delve into whether he has ever been knocked out in his professional career. 

Du Plessis' Fight History

UFC Victories

Du Plessis has had a series of impressive victories in the UFC.

Notably, he won against Markus Perez, Trevin Giles, Brad Tavares, Darren Till, Derek Brunson, and Robert Whittaker. These victories were secured via knockout, decision, and submission, showcasing his versatility as a fighter.

EFC and KSW Achievements

Before making his mark in the UFC, du Plessis had a successful stint in EFC, where he became a two-division champion. Additionally, he had notable wins and losses in KSW, including a victory over Joilton Santos and a loss to Roberto Soldić.

The Question of Knockouts

Knockout History

While du Plessis has exhibited a strong striking game, his professional record does not show any losses by knockout. The available fight history indicates that he has been defeated through submission and decision, but not knockout.

Striking Ability

Du Plessis has demonstrated exceptional striking ability throughout his career, as evidenced by his significant strikes landed per minute (SLpM) and striking accuracy. His prowess in striking, coupled with his defensive skills, has likely contributed to his ability to avoid knockout defeats.

Despite his extensive and successful career in mixed martial arts, Dricus du Plessis has not been knocked out in a professional bout.

His combination of striking prowess, defensive skills, and well-rounded fighting abilities has enabled him to avoid knockout losses.

 

In essence, du Plessis' career trajectory exemplifies his skill, determination, and adaptability as a mixed martial artist, and his ability to avoid knockout defeats adds another layer to his impressive fighting prowess.

While du Plessis has faced various challenges and opponents throughout his career, the fact remains that he has never been knocked out, highlighting his resilience and skill as a fighter.
