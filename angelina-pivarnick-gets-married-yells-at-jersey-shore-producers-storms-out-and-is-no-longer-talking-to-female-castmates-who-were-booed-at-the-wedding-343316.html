Angelina Pivarnick Gets Married: Yells At Jersey Shore Producers, Storms Out, And Is No Longer Talki

Angelina Pivarnick tied the knot to Chris Larangeira yesterday. As you can expect with any Jersey Shore event — this was no regular wedding.

All of her castmates were in attendance when Angelina said ‘I do’ to her fiance — even JWoww and Snooki who she had recently been feuding with. The ladies of the show served as her bridesmaids and the men were also in attendance.

Sources who attended the event say that everything was going off with a hitch until it came time for the speeches. Apparently, Jenni Farley, Nicole Polizzi, and Deena Cortese said somethings that didn’t resonate well with Angelina or the loved ones who were at the wedding.

An insider told US Weekly: ‘Deena, Snooki, and Jenni, in their speeches, made some jokes about Angelina. The audience at the wedding booed them. Their jokes were all in good fun, but they didn’t land and some didn’t think they were kind.’

The source revealed how Pivarnick reacted.
