Penelope Cruz Bio (Wiki)

Who is Penelope Cruz?

Penelope Cruz is a long-haired seductive Spanish actress and model.

She is eminent for her role as a lead actor in the films Vanilla Sky’, ‘All the Pretty Horses, ‘And Captain Corelli’s Mandolin’, and ‘Blow’.

Selected as the 7th Sexiest Female Movie Star in Australian Empire magazine, Penelope got her breakthrough by playing the role of Sofia Serrano in the movie ‘The Vanilla Sky’.

Penelope Cruz: Age(48), Parents, Ethnicity

Penelope was born in Alcobendas, Madrid, Spain on 28 April 1974. She is 48 years old. Her nationality is Spanish and her ethnicity is also Spanish.

Born to Encarna Sanchez, a hairdresser, and personal manager, and Eduardo Cruz, a retailer, and car mechanic.

Penelope Cruz was raised in Alcobendas along with her two siblings Monica and Eduardo as a Roman Catholic.

From a very early age of life, Cruz was interested in acting and modeling which grew up along with her and was pictured as her passion.

Education

To make her passion a profession, she got admitted to learn Classical Ballet for nine years at Spain’s National Conservatory followed by four years of Theatre study at New York School.

Apart from her class in the dancing and acting sector, she participated in various auditions but, she was unable to make her prove that she is really a praiseworthy piece of art.

Penelope Cruz: Professional Life and Career

Though Penelope’s childhood was full of happiness and enjoyment, she had a struggling life to make herself a well-established actor and a model.

After plenty of efforts to be a successful actor, she was selected in one of her auditions beating 300 other girls, and made a debut in the film ‘The Greek Labyrinth’ feature film ‘Jamón, Jamon’ and her debut music video ‘La Fuerza del Destino’.

She then started to taste the experience of success and played in certain hit movies like Cameron Crowe’s ‘Vanilla Sky’ as a love interest of Tom Cruise’s character, Sofia Serrano, ‘Blow’ in which she played the role of Johny Depp’s Character along with ‘All the Pretty Horses’ and ‘Captain Corelli’s Mandolin’.

She made immense popularity through her work with some of the world’s most versatile actors like Tom Cruise and Johny Depp.

She was also ranked #23 on the ‘102 Sexiest Women in the World’, ‘Esquire magazine’s Sexiest Woman Alive in 2014’, ‘ Academy Award’, ‘2,436th Star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame’, ‘Acting Oscar’, two ‘Oscar-winning Best Foreign Language Films’ and is also known for her film ‘Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides (2011)’.

This news was denied by Penelope herself in one of her interviews mentioning that the news is not a true one making it totally a rumor and not a controversy.
