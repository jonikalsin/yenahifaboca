Lucy Hale To Co-Host Dick Clarks New Years Rockin Eve with Ryan Seacrest 2020

Lucy Hale (Mike Rosenthal)

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — Singer and actress Lucy Hale has been announced as the co-host in New York for “Dick Clark’s New Year’s Rockin’ Eve with Ryan Seacrest 2020.”

“As we ring in a new decade and my 15th year hosting the show, I’m so excited to welcome the talented Lucy Hale to the stage with me,” said Seacrest. “It’s going to be a powerhouse year with Billy and Ciara and we can’t wait to celebrate with everyone!”

Hale, who previously served as host for DCP’s New Year’s Eve event in New Orleans, will join Seacrest on the ground this year in Times Square as the ball comes down to mark the end of the year.

Actor, singer, director, composer, and playwright Billy Porter will make his debut this year as the host in New Orleans, while Ciara will return for her third year as host of the Los Angeles New Year’s Eve broadcast.

“Dick Clark’s New Year’s Rockin’ Eve with Ryan Seacrest 2020” is produced by dick clark productions with Ryan Seacrest, Barry Adelman and Mark Bracco serving as executive producers. Larry Klein is the show’s producer.

The New Year’s Eve broadcast begins on December 31st at 8:30 EST on ABC.
