Career

Brooklyn McKnight started her career as a social media star over the internet. In the year 2008, she made her acting debut and made her first on-screen appearance in the comedy movie titled The Adventures of Food Boy. After that, she has been featured in a television show titled Studio C as a guest.

In the year 2017, she made her singing debut and released her debut single titled Dance Like Me along with her twin sister Bailey McKnight, produced by Benny Cassette of Kanye West’s Good Music label. She rose to huge popularity after the release of this single and also got over 6 million views on YouTube.

She also has a YouTube channel named Brooklyn and Bailey McKnight along with her sister on which she posts interesting videos and promotes various popular brands like Lash Next Door and Too Faced Cosmetics. She and her sister made their appearances on the cover pages of several popular magazines like People Magazine’s Vidcon coverage.

Facts and Information

She and her sister are named as the Champions of the United Nations Foundation’s Girl Up Initiative that mainly focuses on the education, health, and safety of girls all over the world.

She has won the People’s Voice Webby Award for Best Social Media Celebrity in the year 2017 and shared the award with her sister. The same year, she has also been listed among Forbes Top Influencers list.
