Biography

Ben Pakulski was born in Toronto, Canada on March 18, 1981. IFBB bodybuilder nicknamed “Pak-Man” who created the bestselling Mi40 brand of muscle building programs. He has crowned Mr. Canada in 2008 and has gone on to place first at bodybuilding events like the 2016 Vancouver Pro Show. He competed against the likes of Jason Poston at the 2015 Arnold Classic. He started weightlifting at Macks Gym in Toronto, Canada at age 15. He received training from influential strength coaches Charles Poliquin and Charles Glass.
