Who plays Hiram and LeRoy, Rachel Berrys gay dads on Glee?


We finally get to meet Rachel Berry (Lea Michele)’s gay dads on Glee!

And one of them is . . .

Jeff Goldblum!!!!

The other one is Brian Stokes Mitchell, a prolific Tony-winning Broadway star, so that’s obviously where she gets her big talent, and even bigger dreams!

Click on over to Entertainment Weekly to see a sneak-peak clip of the dads surprising Rachel and Finn during the Valentine’s Day episode “Heart” after finding out about their wedding plans. They seem surprising nonplussed to discover that their teenage daughter planned to get married behind their backs, and will be performing Cole Porter’s “You’re the Top.”

The episode airs on Valentine’s Day (February 14) and also features the co-winners of The Glee Project Samuel Larsen and Damian McGinty performing Gym Class Heroes and Adam Levine’s duet “Stereo Hearts” and Michael Buble’s “Home.”
