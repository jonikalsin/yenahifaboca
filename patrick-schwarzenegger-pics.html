Patrick Schwarzenegger: Photos Hollywood Life

See photos of Patrick Schwarzenegger, the eldest son of Arnold Schwarzenegger and Maria Shriver.

Patrick Schwarzenegger is an American actor and model (signed with LA Models) was born on September 18. 1993. He is the son of famed parents, Austrian-born bodybuilder, actor, and former Republican Governor of California, Arnold Schwarzenegger and journalist Maria Shriver who is part of the Kennedy family. At the age of ten, he had a small role in the movie ‘The Benchwarmers’.  In 2012, he enrolled at the University of Southern California, and later graduated from the USC Marshall School of Business with a minor in Cinematic Arts in May 2016. Patrick was part of the Lambda Chi Alpha fraternity. He’s had supporting roles in ‘Stuck in Love’ (2012), ‘Grown Ups 2’ (2013) and ‘Scouts Guide to the Zombie Apocalypse’ (2015). Patrick’s first leading role came in 2018, when he starred alongside Bella Thorne in ‘Midnight Sun’, a romantic drama about a teenage girl with a rare medical condition. He played Bella’s love interest in the film. He’s appeared in the television shows ‘Scream Queens’ (2015) and ‘The Long Road Home’.

Patrick previously dated Miley Cyrus. He got engaged to model Abby Champion in December 2023. See more photos of Patrick in our gallery.
