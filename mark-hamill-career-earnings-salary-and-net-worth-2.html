Mark Hamill career earnings, salary and net worth

Mark Richard Hamill is a writer and actor from the United States. He is well-known for his character portrayal of Luke Skywalker in the Star Wars film series, starting with the first movie in 1977, for which he went on to win three Saturn Awards.

Mark Richard Hamill was born to a U.S. Navy Captain named William Thomas Hamill and his wife Virginia Suzanne in Oakland, California, on September 25, 1951.

Hamill attended Nile C. Kinnick High School, where he received his diploma in 1969, and participated in the Drama Club there. Later, he chose to major in drama at Los Angeles City College.

Early on in his acting career, Hamill appeared often on the soap opera General Hospital and starred in the ephemeral sitcom The Texas Wheelers.
