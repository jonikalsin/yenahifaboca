How to Watch Framing Britney Spears Documentary On Hulu

Listen up: it’s Britney, bitch. This time, however, you’ll get to see her life and controversies chronicled like you’ve never seen before. In “Framing Britney Spears,” one of the most well-known and misunderstood celebrity narratives of our time is explored in detail and the beloved Britney Spears is at the center of this investigation.

We’ve all been hearing about “Framing Britney Spears” for some time now, but when you search Hulu, nothing seems to come up. Wondering how to watch “Framing Britney Spears” on Hulu? Here’s everything you need to know.

When Did Framing Britney Spears Premiere?

“Framing Britney Spears” premiered as the sixth episode of FX‘s The New York Times Presents series on Friday, February 5. The episode was released on FX and subsequently on Hulu.

From FX, The New York Times Presents is a series of documentaries all from one of the most well-known news organizations in the world. The popular series explores everything from medical professionals on the frontlines of COVID-19 to the killing of Breonna Taylor.

What Is Framing Britney Spears About?

Remember the Leave Britney Alone guy? It turns out he was right. Framing Britney Spears takes our current understanding of Spears’ rise to fame and questions it. Why was her father allowed to control so much of her finances? Why were we as a society so obsessed with this woman’s sexuality, especially when she was underage? And, most chillingly of all, why were we so excited to see her fall? Faming Britney Spears attempts to answer these questions while reintroducing humanity and compassion to this well-known celebrity story.

How to Watch Framing Britney Spears on Hulu

If you don’t have a Hulu account but want to get one, it’s pretty simple. Subscriptions to the streaming service start at $5.99 a month for the basic plan. But what if you hate commercials? Then the $11.99 a month no ads plan is for you.

Also, Hulu + Live TV and Hulu (No Ads) + Live TV both come with basic Hulu, so you can hop on over to the platform’s streaming library to watch The New York Times Presents and select Episode 6 “Framing Britney Spears” if you have either of those subscriptions. The streaming service even offers the option to try before you buy. Seven-day free trials are available to new subscribers and other eligible customers.

Where Else Can You Watch Framing Britney Spears?

While The New York Times Presents premiered on FX and FX on Hulu, the series is currently only available on Hulu. But if you’re not seeing it in your search, make sure to look for the actual full title of the series, The New York Times Presents, which after selecting will allow you to choose Episode 6 “Framing Britney Spears.”

If you have the TV skinny bundle Hulu + Live TV then you should be all set to stream the series as well since that bundle comes with Hulu’s vast library of content. You can also head to the FXNOW platform to stream the docuseries as of June 4, 2021.

Because of FX’s partnership with Hulu, all new episodes of FX shows appear on Hulu either immediately or soon after their premiere time. That means as long as you have a Hulu account, you’re currently free to watch people defend Britney Spears along with the rest of the world.
